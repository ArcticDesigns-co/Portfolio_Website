Graphic / Web Design - Photo Editing

The ask for this project was to design a logo for a coffee shop and put that logo on three dimensional objects. For the logo I choose to do a teacup outline with a tea bag. I wanted to leave the outlines with a rounded edge to make the design more comforting and cozy. The outer circles main purpose was to draw attention towards the inter logo and to distinguish itself from the object it was placed on. The renders themselves were three items that I think you would find in a shop like this, a coffee bag, an aluminum can, and a coffee cup. I also choose a slightly tan lighting to again give the render that cozy feeling to it.
