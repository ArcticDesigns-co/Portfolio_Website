Graphic / Web Design - Photo Editing

This is my site logo, the inspiration behind this logo was that I like cold weather animals such as arctic foxes, polar bears, and penguins. I originally had arctic fox for my logo but I didn't keep it as it lost a large amount of detail when scaled to ~150 pixels or fewer. For this design I wanted to keep that same theme of cold weather so I went with deep blues and white to contrast it to mimic snow. Near the top middle I added a star which represents the north star to tie in the idea that this is the arctic. And the light blue water at the bottom was to give the mountains more depth. For this design the only change I would make is center the star to the middle of the whole design instead of the middle of the mountain that it is above.
